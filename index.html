Popular Destinations

Tadoba National Park

Notably Maharashtra's oldest and largest National Park, the "Tadoba National Park", also known as the "Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve" is one of India's 47 project tiger reserves existing in India.

Bamboo Prashikshan Kendra

The Bamboo Research and Training Centre located in Chandrapur, a tribal area in Maharashtra is conceived as one of Asia's largest training centre built in (locally available) Bamboo.

Mokda Shwas

Ek Mokala Shwas is an agricultural and rural tourism hub. It is located in the village of Chanakha, just 8 km from Rajura.Attempts have been made to show rural life in this tourist center.


Seven Sister Hill

Seven sisters lived on this mountain named Ambai, Nimbai, Muktai, Lakshmi, Saraswati, Mahakali and Durga and some local people told me that these sisters used to live in the caves of the hill in ancient times.
