# Tour-To-Chanda
 **Chandrapur Tourism Website** is a travel guide showcasing the district's top attractions, including Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve, historic Gond forts, temples, waterfalls, lakes, local culture, hotels, restaurants, and shopping destinations. It helps visitors explore Chandrapur's rich heritage, wildlife, and tourism offerings in one place.

## Chandrapur Tourism Website

**Chandrapur Tourism Website** is a comprehensive travel guide designed to showcase the rich heritage, natural beauty, and cultural diversity of Chandrapur district, Maharashtra. This website serves as a one-stop platform for tourists, travelers, and history enthusiasts who wish to explore the district's most famous attractions and hidden gems.

The platform provides detailed information about renowned destinations such as Tadoba-Andhari Tiger Reserve, ancient Gond-era forts, historic temples, scenic waterfalls, serene lakes, and culturally significant landmarks. Visitors can also discover the unique traditions, architecture, and heritage of the Gond Kingdom that played a major role in shaping the region's history.

In addition to tourist attractions, the website offers practical travel information, including recommended hotels, restaurants, shopping destinations, and local experiences, helping visitors plan their trip efficiently. With an intuitive design, engaging visuals, and informative content, the website aims to promote tourism and highlight the historical, cultural, and ecological significance of Chandrapur.

### Key Features

* Information about major tourist attractions and historical sites
* Dedicated sections for forts, temples, waterfalls, lakes, and wildlife destinations
* Insights into Gond culture, traditions, and heritage
* Hotel and accommodation recommendations
* Restaurant and local food guides
* Shopping and local market information
* User-friendly interface with attractive visuals
* Educational and travel-oriented content for visitors

### Purpose

The primary objective of this project is to promote Chandrapur as a tourist destination by providing visitors with accurate, organized, and engaging information about its natural wonders, historical monuments, cultural heritage, and hospitality services.

**Built with:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
