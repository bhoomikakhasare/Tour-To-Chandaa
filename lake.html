Beautiful Lakes in Chandrapur

Ramala Lake

Ramala Lake is a historic lake in Chandrapur built during the Gond dynasty. It is known for its scenic beauty, peaceful atmosphere, and cultural significance.

Junona Lake

Junona Lake is a serene waterbody located near Chandrapur city. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and bird watchers.

Ghodajhari Lake

Ghodajhari Lake is a beautiful reservoir surrounded by lush greenery. It is famous for its picturesque views and tranquil environment.

Lohara Lake

Lohara Lake is a peaceful tourist spot situated near Chandrapur. The lake offers scenic landscapes and is ideal for relaxation and photography.
