Zari M is a small Village/hamlet in Chimur Taluka in Chandrapur District of Maharashtra State, India. It comes under Zari M Panchayath. It belongs to Vidarbh region . It belongs to Nagpur Division . It is located 72 KM towards North from District head quarters Chandrapur. 824 KM from State capital Mumbai Zari M is surrounded by Bhivapur Taluka towards North , Nagbhid Taluka towards East , Sindewahi Taluka towards South , Samudrapur Taluka towards west .

The village Zari is located in Chimur Tahsil of Chandrapur District in the State of Maharashtra in India. It comes under Chimur Community Development Block.
