About Chandrapur

Chandrapur, formerly known as Chanda, is a historic district in eastern Maharashtra. Famous for its coal reserves, rich forests, wildlife, and cultural heritage, it is often called the Black Gold City.

Quick Facts

  • 📍 State: Maharashtra
  • 🌳 Home to Tadoba Tiger Reserve
  • ⛏️ Major Coal Producing Region
  • 📐 Area: 11,443 km²

How To Reach

  • ✈️ Nearest Airport: Nagpur
  • 🚆 Well Connected by Rail Network
  • 🚌 Direct Bus Routes from Major Cities

Major Attractions

  • 🐅 Tadoba National Park
  • 🏞️ Seven Sister Hill
  • 🎋 Bamboo Research Centre
  • 🌿 Rural Tourism Villages
